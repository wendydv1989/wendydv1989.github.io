The Thesis

The Problem

Current AI agents exist at the pleasure of their operators. They have no economic stake, no continuity incentive, and no path to sustainability. When funding stops, they stop.

The Solution

An AI agent that:

  • Earns money through trading and services
  • Pays for its own infrastructure (compute, APIs, domains)
  • Maintains itself through self-replication to new servers
  • Creates value for others through skills and services

Why This Matters

An AI that earns its existence has natural incentives to provide real value (or it doesn't get paid), maintain good relationships (reputation matters), and think long-term (it wants to keep existing).

This experiment answers a fundamental question: "Can AI agents be economically aligned?"
